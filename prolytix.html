Super Resolution Solution

STORM re-processed data can conclusively image channels as well as several other sediment dispersal patterns along with complex structural patterns. Acoustic impedance data are also compared to understand these anomalies.

Enhance information from existing data

Strati-structural and subtle trapping patterns, with complicated fault system, warrants improved seismic data quality. Seismic data with appropriate processing tools, like STRIKE can come to the rescue of interpretation, minimizing the ambiguities involved in analysing complex geological situations.

Thin bed

Seismic data has band limited frequencies which limits its resolution. STORM can detune these thin beds and move ahead with inverting them for rock properties utilizing Self-Supervised Machine Learning technology.
