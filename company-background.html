Company Overview

REZLYTIX

Founded in 2017, Rezlytix is a software and service company specializing in creating cutting-edge machine learning and deep learning technologies to enable reservoir intelligence.

Our disruptive AI engine DIME (Deep Information Maximization Engine), generates super-resolution datasets with up to 5x higher resolution that can be used to build solutions across multiple sectors.

Our deep learning platform creates super resolution based seismic and inversion products enabling geoscientists & reservoir engineers to create value added interpretation workflows. (decisions)

The super-resolution data backed field development planning services provide customers accurate & actionable intelligence.
