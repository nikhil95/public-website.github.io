Solutions

Super Resolution backed upscaled reservoir models increase confidence & provide more actionable insight.



  • Imaging & Data processing
  • Interpretation & Modelling
  • Bidding and Field development planning
  • Software development consultancy

Imaging & Data processing

  • Reprocessing and imaging with tailor-made structural enhancement for better super-resolution.
  • Pre-stack gathers super-resolution for more accurate reservoir characterization.

Interpretation & Modelling

  • Rock property models at super-resolution for more accurate reservoir simulations.
  • Relative geologic time based fine-grained and bias-free structural and stratigraphic interpretation.
  • Multi-attribute analysis along super-resolution stratal slices for improved petroleum system analysis and reservoir delineation.
  • Make the most of existing FWI based apriori models in complex salt scenarios.

Bidding and Field development planning

  • Volumetric assessment of leftover and recoverable hydrocarbon-in-place.
  • Identify and rank optimized well paths, recompletion strategies, vertical sweet spots and horizontal wells to lower costs and work towards net-zero goals.
  • Asset evaluation and business case preparation for bringing in new investment.

Software development consultancy

  • Bespoke application/algorithm development on top of our deep learning platform, utilizing Lucy, our Deep Information Maximization Engine (DIME)
  • Integrate our product or solutions into your existing workflow.
  • Migrate our solutions and products to your platform of choice.
